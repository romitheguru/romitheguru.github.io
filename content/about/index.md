I am Romee Panchal, an AI engineer with more than nine years of experience across software engineering and data science.

I am drawn to difficult technical problems and to the equally difficult work of explaining them clearly. This blog is where I connect those interests: unpacking machine learning systems, tracing how ideas evolve, and turning practical experience into useful mental models.

You will find essays about machine learning, natural language processing, deep learning, data science, and the craft of learning technical subjects well. My aim is not to repeat documentation. It is to explain why an approach works, where it breaks, and how to think about the trade-offs.

Outside the blog, you can find me on [X](https://twitter.com/romitheguru), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/romeepanchal/), and [GitHub](https://github.com/romitheguru).
